Biography

Huaxi Huang is now a CERC Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the CSIRO’s Imaging and Computer Vision Group (ICV) and the USYD’s Trustworthy Machine Learning Lab (TML Lab). He received his Ph.D. in 2022 from the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). From 2010 to 2017, Huaxi earned his M.Eng. degree and B.Eng. degree from the school of computer science and the school of computer software engineering, Tianjin University. His research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision, especially few label learning, noise label learning, and differential privacy preservation learning.
