Huaxi Huang

CERC Research Fellow

Data61, CSIRO

Biography

Huaxi Huang is currently a CERC Postdoctoral Research Fellow in CSIRO’s Imaging and Computer Vision Group (ICV) and the Sydney AI Centre, under the supervision of Prof. Dadong Wang and Prof. Tongliang Liu. He was awarded his Ph.D. from the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in 2022. From 2010 to 2017, Huaxi earned his B.Eng. degree and M.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Software Engineering and the School of Computer Science and Technology, Tianjin University. His research interests lie in multimedia, computer vision, and trustworthy machine learning, especially few-label learning, noisy-label learning, and privacy preservation learning.
